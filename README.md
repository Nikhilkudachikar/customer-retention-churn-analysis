## Task Overview

This project focuses on Customer Retention and Churn Analysis for a subscription-based business. The objective is to analyze customer data to identify patterns that lead to customer churn and understand factors that influence customer retention.

The analysis helps businesses understand why customers leave, how long customers stay with the service, and what actions can improve long-term customer engagement. Using tools such as Power BI, Excel, or Python, the dataset is cleaned, analyzed, and visualized to generate meaningful business insights.

The final outcome of this project is a dashboard or analytical report highlighting churn trends, retention performance, and actionable strategies to reduce customer loss.

## Key Insights

- A portion of customers stop using the service after a specific period, indicating a clear churn pattern.
- Certain customer segments show a higher churn rate compared to others.
- Customers with shorter subscription durations tend to leave more frequently.
- Some service plans or pricing structures are associated with higher retention rates.
- Customer engagement levels strongly influence whether customers stay or leave.

- ## Recommendations

- Improve onboarding experience for new customers to increase early engagement.
- Offer loyalty programs or incentives for long-term subscribers.
- Identify high-risk customers and implement targeted retention strategies.
- Optimize pricing plans and service offerings to better match customer needs.
- Continuously monitor churn metrics to detect issues early and improve customer satisfaction.
